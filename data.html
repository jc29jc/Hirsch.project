The Data Hub (The Numbers)

Welcome to the data hub. Now that we have looked at the every-day struggles on the Inequity page, now we can bring in the evidence. This page will show the numbers and evidence, backing up the problems on the last page with facts, statistics, and data. Here are the four main questions that will be answered on this page:

1. What is the unemployment rate for people with a record compared to everyone else?

2. How many people end up getting arrested again within three to five years of being released?

3. How much tax money goes into running prisons vs funding programs that help people rebuild their lives?

4.How does putting so many people in jail end up costing our communities money?
