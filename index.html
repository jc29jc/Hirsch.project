// Introduction

When someone gets out of prison, people usually like to believe that their punishment has been faced and that they can just start fresh. But in reality, instead of being free to start over, they face a new invisible punishment that no one recognizes after being released. This website is going to expose the harsh reality of the challenges an ex-convict faces after being released from jail. So, now dive into the site to learn about the traps and struggles behind ex-convicts, the evidence, and see how our community can fight for fair second chances for these people.
