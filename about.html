04 // About Us

Welcome to the Our Team page. This page is about me and my partner and why we started this project. We wanted to look into a big problem that many people don't know about. Here you will see who we are, why we care about this cause, and how we plan to help ex-convicts get a fair second chance.

Who We Are

JACOB

Co-Founder

My name is Jacob, and I am a 16 year-old freshman at Redwood High, where a huge part of my life revolves around hanging out with friends and playing sports like soccer and golf. Being on school and club teams has taught me a ton about fairness, helping people, how coming together as a team or group can help elevate everyone, and how important it is for everyone to have a chance to prove themself. Outside of sports, I decided to create this website because it stood out to me how unfair things are for ex-convicts. When someone leaves prison, they are supposed to have a fresh start, but instead, they run into problems like being unable to get a safe place to live or a decent job. I wanted to do this project because I don't think a past mistake should ruin someone's life. By showing people the struggles ex-convicts face everyday, I think I can help change the bad stereotypes and build a stronger community that believes in giving people a second chance.

ISAAC

Co-Founder

My name is Isaac and I am a 15 year old freshman at Redwood High School. Just like Jacob, a big part of my life is surrounded by sports like soccer, hanging out with friends, and partying. Sports and social groups have also taught me how much your friends and community matter when you're trying to succeed, and how everyone needs to build a solid support system around them. The reason I decided to help with this website is because it had always bothered me how people judge someone based on their worst mistake even if it was years in the past. When someone gets out of jail, they should have the opportunity to change, but instead, people immediately shut them off because of their reputation. I wanted to do this project because I think that when we don't give people fair chances to turn their lives around, it hurts the whole community. I think that if I start to try and make a difference, more people will too, and it will really help these ex-convicts out.

Our Plan To Help

First, we built this website to serve as a “home base” for our project. This website is a place where anyone can read real data and facts about what ex-convicts face, and find resources to help take action. This includes putting ideas out on the web to our plans of action like our podcast and instagram so others can be influenced by us to do the same.

Next, we created an instagram account to put our ideas out to the scrolling world and the younger people. Through insta we can share quick facts, statistics, and more of people trying to rebuild their lives after prison.

Finally, our podcast is meant for us to sit down and have long, real conversations about this problem. We are hoping that these platforms and ideas together will show people that ex-convicts deserve better and shouldn't have a harder experience of rebuilding their life. Everyone deserves a fair second chance and we need more people to realize that.
