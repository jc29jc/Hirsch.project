The Inequity

Welcome to the Inequity page. Before we jump into numbers or statistics, we need to understand the human side of this problem. When someone leaves prison, everyday tasks that seem simple to us like finding a good place to sleep or getting a job turn into huge challenges for these ex-convicts. This page will break down the challenges that returning citizens face when they get out of prison.

There are four major questions that we will be looking at:

1. What hard struggles do people face the exact moment they get out of prison?

2. How does having a criminal record ruin someone's chances of finding a place to live or getting a job, and how?

3. How does society focusing on punishing people instead of helping them change hurt their chances of a fresh start, and how?

4. How does discrimination against ex-convicts lead to systemic exclusion?
