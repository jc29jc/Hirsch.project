    National Movements & Protests

    Ban the Box

    The ban the box movement supports the idea that job applications shouldn't have questions about former criminal history, so that formerly incarcerated people have the same chance of getting a job as the general public.

    Second Chance Act

    The Second Chance Act (SCA) is the largest source of federal funding for reentry. It provides grants to state and local governments and nonprofits to offer employment training, housing, substance abuse treatment, and mentoring.

    Other Inspiring Movements

    If you want to look into other national movements, you can check out Blackstone Career Institute's list of inspiring organizations. By looking at different organizations, it shows that people are really fighting for a change. And this ultimately leads to these ex-convicts getting better support and housing and jobs so they can successfully rebuild their lives.
