Take Action

This is the Taking Action page. Just talking about a problem or looking at statistics does not actually fix anything, so we need to talk about solutions. This page will discuss how people are fighting back to change this inequality. We will look at the big protests and movements working to rewrite laws and beliefs, groups helping out in Marin County, and everyday things people can do to help.

Here are the four main questions about solutions that will be answered on this page:

1. What group strategy's or national movements exist to help ex-convicts?

2. What is the Ban the Box movement and what are its goals?

3. What are good ways for communities to support prisoner second chances?

4. What legal help and reentry services does the New Beginnings Law Center provide in Marin County?
