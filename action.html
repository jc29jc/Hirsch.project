03 // Taking Action

Just talking about a problem doesn't fix it, so this page is all about solutions and what everybody can do. We are going to talk about big national movements and protests trying to change laws behind this problem, and actual groups helping here in Marin County, and finally, showing different simple ways people can help out everyday.

// Solution Action Plans

(Click on an action box below to expand the solutions directly underneath)

  • 01

    National Movements & Protests

    Changing the Laws

    Large-scale national advocacy groups push for legal updates like "Ban the Box" policies, which remove the criminal record check box from initial job applications so people can be judged on their skills first.

  • 02

    Local Support in Marin County

    Community Networks

    Right here in Marin County, there are community programs and non-profits dedicated to helping returning citizens find stable housing, access mental health support, and secure steady employment.

  • 03

    Everyday Support Solutions

    Simple Ways to Help

    Regular citizens can assist by supporting fair-chance employers, volunteering with tutoring or mentorship programs, and spreading accurate information to counter harmful stereotypes.
